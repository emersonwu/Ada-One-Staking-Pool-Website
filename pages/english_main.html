Why Choose Us?

ONE1 is a responsible Stake Pool that aims to support Cardano to achieve decentralization by installing our own physical servers and nodes in order to avoid recentralization in few cloud service providers. By staking your ADA in ONE1 you are helping to support the real physical decentralization of the network contributing to a new, more equitable world. We will also donate part of the reward to support ONE campaign against extreme poverty in the world.

Safety

Cardano addresses have separate keys for spending and staking. This means that if you decide to stake your ADA, they will never leave your wallet or be locked in for a term, you can un-stake at any time.

Rewards

The annual rewards of staking is on average between 5% and 6% in ADA, as we hope that “The world will be as ONE”, we will donate part of our rewards to support One Campaign, an international, non-partisan, non-profit organization. for profit, advocacy and campaigns to combat extreme poverty and preventable diseases, particularly in Africa.
